SCHEME: PM-KISAN

Benefits:
Eligible farmers receive ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments directly into their bank accounts.

Eligibility:
Small and marginal farmers who own cultivable land.

Required Documents:
- Aadhaar Card
- Land ownership records
- Bank account details

Application Process:
Apply through the PM-KISAN portal or visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC).

Official Source:
https://pmkisan.gov.in


SCHEME: Ayushman Bharat

Benefits:
Provides health insurance coverage of up to ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary healthcare.

Eligibility:
Economically vulnerable families identified under government guidelines.

Required Documents:
- Aadhaar Card
- Ration Card
- Mobile Number

Application Process:
Visit an empanelled hospital or Ayushman Bharat help centre.

Official Source:
https://pmjay.gov.in


SCHEME: PMAY (Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana)

Benefits:
Provides financial assistance for constructing or purchasing a house.

Eligibility:
Families without a permanent (pucca) house and belonging to eligible income groups.

Required Documents:
- Aadhaar Card
- Income Certificate
- Residence Proof

Application Process:
Apply through local authorities or the official PMAY portal.

Official Source:
https://pmaymis.gov.in


SCHEME: Ujjwala Yojana

Benefits:
Provides free LPG connections to eligible households.

Eligibility:
Women belonging to eligible low-income households.

Required Documents:
- Aadhaar Card
- Ration Card
- Bank Account Details

Application Process:
Apply through authorised LPG distributors.

Official Source:
https://pmuy.gov.in


SCHEME: MGNREGA

Benefits:
Provides guaranteed wage employment for up to 100 days in a financial year.

Eligibility:
Adult members of rural households willing to do unskilled manual work.

Required Documents:
- Aadhaar Card
- Job Card
- Residence Proof

Application Process:
Register at the Gram Panchayat office.

Official Source:
https://nrega.nic.in


SCHEME: Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Benefits:
Offers a high-interest savings scheme for the education and marriage expenses of girl children.

Eligibility:
Parents or guardians of a girl child below 10 years of age.

Required Documents:
- Birth Certificate of the child
- Aadhaar Card of parent/guardian

Application Process:
Open an account at a post office or authorised bank.

Official Source:
https://nsiindia.gov.in


SCHEME: PM Shram Yogi Maandhan

Benefits:
Provides a minimum assured monthly pension of ₹3,000 after the age of 60 years.

Eligibility:
Unorganised workers aged between 18 and 40 years meeting prescribed income criteria.

Required Documents:
- Aadhaar Card
- Savings Bank Account Details
- Mobile Number

Application Process:
Enroll through Common Service Centres (CSCs).

Official Source:
https://maandhan.in


SCHEME: PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)

Benefits:
Provides life insurance coverage of ₹2 lakh.

Eligibility:
Individuals aged between 18 and 50 years having a savings bank account.

Required Documents:
- Aadhaar Card
- Bank Account Details

Application Process:
Enroll through participating banks.

Official Source:
https://jansuraksha.gov.in
